Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Baby Activity Planner. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you use our mobile application and tell you about your privacy rights.

Information We Collect

We are committed to minimal data collection and maximum privacy. Here's what you should know about our data practices:

  • Analytics Data: We use Mixpanel analytics software solely to count the total number of users. This is the only data that leaves your device. The analytics data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify individual users.
  • Local Storage: All app data, including child age settings, play suggestions, and app preferences, is stored exclusively on your device. We do not have access to this information, and it never leaves your device.
  • Calendar Integration: When you save play suggestions to your calendar, this is done directly through your device's calendar app. We do not have access to your calendar data, and calendar events are stored locally on your device.
  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information. We don't require accounts, email addresses, or any other personal information to use the app.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Mixpanel Analytics: We use Mixpanel analytics software to count the total number of users. This is the only data transmitted from your device. The analytics data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify individual users.

How We Use Your Information

Our data usage is minimal and transparent:

  • Analytics: We use Mixpanel analytics software solely to count the total number of users. This helps us understand how many people are using the app, but we cannot identify individual users from this data.
  • Local Data: All data stored on your device is used only to provide the app's core functionality. This includes:
    • Storing child age settings
    • Generating play suggestions based on your settings
    • Storing your app preferences
    • Maintaining your local app data
  • Calendar Integration: When you choose to save a play suggestion to your calendar, the app uses your device's calendar integration. Calendar events are created and stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your calendar data.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share any data with third parties, except for anonymized total user count data sent to Mixpanel for analytics purposes.

Data Security

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities:

  • All app data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage systems
  • No personal data or app content is transmitted from your device, except for anonymized total user count to Mixpanel
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect analytics data in transit
  • You have complete control over your data - you can delete the app at any time to remove all local data
  • Calendar events are stored locally on your device and managed by your device's calendar app

Your Data Protection Rights

Since we store all data locally on your device, you have complete control over your information:

  • You can delete the app at any time to remove all local data
  • All play suggestions and child age settings are stored only on your device
  • Analytics data (total user count) is collected anonymously through Mixpanel. This data cannot be linked to individual users and is used in aggregate form only.
  • If you have any concerns about your privacy or data usage, please contact us through the methods listed below.

Analytics and Data Collection

We use Mixpanel analytics software for one purpose only:

  • To count the total number of users
  • All analytics data is collected anonymously
  • We cannot identify individual users from the analytics data
  • No personal information, child age settings, play suggestions, or calendar data is transmitted
  • Analytics data is used in aggregate form only

Children's Privacy

Our service is designed for parents and caregivers. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. All data entered in the app, including child age settings, is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to us or any third party (except for anonymized total user count to Mixpanel).

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
