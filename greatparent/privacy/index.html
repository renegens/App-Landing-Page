Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Great Parent. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you use our mobile application and tell you about your privacy rights.

Information We Collect

We are committed to minimal data collection and maximum privacy. Here's what you should know about our data practices:

  • Analytics Data: We use analytics software to understand how our app is used. This includes basic usage patterns, app performance metrics, and anonymized chat questions. This data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify individual users.
  • Chat Data: When you use the chat feature, your questions are processed through a third-party AI chat provider. We do not store your chat history locally, but anonymized chat questions are included in our analytics to help improve the service. The chat data is not linked to any personal identifiers and is used solely to provide responses to your questions.
  • Local Storage: All other data, including your baby's information, tracking data, and app preferences, is stored exclusively on your device. We do not have access to this information, and it never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it.
  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information. We don't require accounts, email addresses, or any other personal information to use the app.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • AI Chat Provider:
    • We use a third-party AI chat provider to provide chat-based assistance in our app
    • Your chat questions are sent to the provider's servers for processing
    • We do not store your chat history on our servers
    • Anonymized chat questions are included in our analytics to help improve the service
    • Chat data is not linked to any personal identifiers
    • We cannot control how the AI chat provider handles or stores this data
  • Analytics Software: As described in our Analytics section, we use analytics software to improve our app's performance and user experience, including analyzing anonymized chat questions to improve our responses.

How We Use Your Information

Our data usage is minimal and transparent:

  • Analytics: We use analytics software solely to improve our app's performance and user experience. This helps us understand which features are most useful, identify areas for improvement, and analyze anonymized chat questions to enhance our responses.
  • Chat Feature: Your chat questions are used to provide immediate responses through our AI chat provider. While we don't store chat history, anonymized questions are included in our analytics to help improve the service.
  • Local Data: All data stored on your device is used only to provide the app's core functionality. This includes:
    • Tracking your baby's development milestones
    • Storing your preferences and settings
    • Maintaining your local app data
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share any data with third parties, except for:
    • Anonymized analytics data (including chat questions) sent to our analytics provider
    • Chat questions sent to our AI chat provider for processing

Data Security

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities:

  • All data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage systems
  • We use secure infrastructure for analytics data
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any data in transit
  • You have complete control over your data - you can delete the app at any time to remove all local data

Your Data Protection Rights

Since we store all data locally on your device, you have complete control over your information:

  • You can delete the app at any time to remove all local data
  • Analytics data is collected anonymously through our analytics software to help us improve the app. This data cannot be linked to individual users and is used in aggregate form only.
  • If you have any concerns about your privacy or data usage, please contact us through the methods listed below.

Analytics and Data Collection

We use analytics software to improve our app:

  • All analytics data is collected anonymously
  • We cannot identify individual users from the analytics data
  • Analytics helps us understand which features are most useful and how we can improve the app
  • Analytics data is used in aggregate form only

Children's Privacy

Our service is designed for parents and caregivers. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
